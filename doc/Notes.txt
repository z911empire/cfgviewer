SELECT P.ConfigurationRecord
FROM ProcessConfiguration P, Process PR, ApplicationType A
WHERE PR.ProcessNameID=P.ProcessNameID AND A.ApplicationTypeID=PR.ApplicationTypeID
  AND P.ConfigurationType='MCastAppFeedInstanceConfig' AND P.IsActive=1 AND A.Name='MarketDataDistributor';


SELECT P.ConfigurationType, P.ConfigurationRecord, PR.ProcessName
FROM ProcessConfiguration P, Process PR, ApplicationType A
WHERE PR.ProcessNameID=P.ProcessNameID AND A.ApplicationTypeID=PR.ApplicationTypeID
  AND P.IsActive=1 AND A.Name='MarketDataDistributor'
ORDER BY PR.ProcessName, P.ConfigurationType;

Process-level entries, if they exist, are overlayed on the template to generate the actual configuration.
	This is what a delete of the 'messaging' value of AppShellConfig looks like in a process-specific entry:
	
	<?xml version="1.0"?>
	<structure type="ApplicationShellConfig">
		<logLevels>
			<logFacility value="messaging"/>
		</logLevels>
	</structure>
	
	The process value represents the delta.
